Export & Outputs

GeoJSON (Vector Overlays)

Exports cells/instances as standardized Polygon or MultiPolygon geometries. Includes detailed feature properties embedding specific cell metrics for interoperability.

CSV (Cell-Level Metrics)

Tabular export of extracted metrics including centroid coordinates, area, mean intensity, predicted class, and confidence scores for downstream statistical analysis.

JSON (Structured Outputs)

Comprehensive structured export containing job metadata, session parameters, and summary statistics ensuring complete provenance of the analysis run.

ROI Image Export (PNG/TIFF)

High-resolution raster snapshots of selected ROIs, available both as raw images and with rendered segmentation overlays for publication and review.
