Solution Overview: VitaminPScope

End-to-end platform for AI-powered analysis

Brings powerful computational pathology and machine learning models directly to Whole-Slide Images (WSI).

Scalable whole-slide and ROI inference

Seamlessly process targeted Regions of Interest (ROIs) or full slides with interactive, real-time visual overlays.

Modern, deployable microservices architecture

Driven by clean REST APIs and Dockerized components, fully functional in HPC or restricted offline environments.

Core design goals

Built meticulously for maximum reliability, processing speed, clinical usability, and absolute reproducibility.
