Motivation

Whole-cell segmentation from H&E is intrinsically hard

Stain variability, complex tissue morphology, and preparation artifacts degrade naive ML model performance.

WSI scale: 10,000–100,000+ pixels per side

Memory constraints make full-image naive inference entirely infeasible without complex tiling and orchestration.

Need for scalable, interactive tools

Researchers and clinicians require platforms that seamlessly bridge experimental ML models and intuitive visualization.

Existing gaps in computational pathology

Lack of reproducible pipelines, robust GPU-aware serving, and clinician-friendly interactive exploration tools.
