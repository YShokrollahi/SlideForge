Performance & Scalability

Tile-Based Processing

Configurable overlap and dynamic tiling. Streams I/O directly from WSI pyramids to minimize memory overhead.

Asynchronous Jobs

Non-blocking architecture allows concurrent ROI and slide processing. UI remains responsive during heavy computation.

GPU Batching & Precision

Optimized batched inference with mixed precision on GPU. Built-in backpressure handling when running in CPU-only mode.

Whole-Slide Scalability

Scales effortlessly from small targeted ROIs to massive full-slide analyses. Jobs are resumable on failure.
