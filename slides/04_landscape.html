Where VitaminPScope fits in the current landscape

The goal is not to replace every pathology platform. It is to provide a focused bridge between model development and interactive deployment.

Public / open-source platforms

Examples: QuPath, Cytomine, Digital Slide Archive

Strengths:
• Strong viewer and analysis ecosystems
• Flexible and community-driven

Typical gap for us:
• More custom work needed for our model-specific workflow

Commercial platforms

Examples: HALO, Proscia, Aiforia

Strengths:
• Polished enterprise workflows
• Larger product surface area

Typical gap for us:
• Less tailored to rapid internal model iteration

VitaminPScope

Focus:
• Direct integration with VitaminP
• ROI + WSI inference workflows
• Research-friendly exports
• Fast iteration by our own team

Tradeoff:
• Narrower scope than full enterprise ecosystems
